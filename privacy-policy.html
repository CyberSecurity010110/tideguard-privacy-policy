Overview

TideGuard is a paid mobile app that does not collect, store, or share personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what (if any) data TideGuard collects and how it is handled.

Information Collection and Use

TideGuard does not collect personal data. The app does not request network, location, or any permissions that would send information off the device. All settings (for example the tide reference time) are stored locally on the user's device and never transmitted to external servers.

Log Data and Analytics

We do not collect log data, analytics, or use third-party tracking services or advertising SDKs.

Third-Party Services

TideGuard does not use third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data. If that changes in future versions of the app, this policy will be updated and the Play Console listing will be updated accordingly.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when changes are made. Please review this page periodically.
